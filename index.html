Hi. We are KKB48.

And this is our project, PH Twitter Fake News Analysis: Gabriela partylist is a front of the terrorist group CPP-NPA, which explores the prevalence of mis/disinformation present in social media regarding the redtagging of the Gabriela Women’s Partylist.

Here's an overview of our project.

Motivation

The prevalence of mis/disinformation about the involvement of political parties that are vastly present on social media platforms has been a growing concern in recent years.

Problem

Mis/Disinformation related to these political parties can have a significant impact on public opinion and decision-making processes.

Solution

This project hopes to shed light on the extent of the prevalence of mis/disinformation by scraping tweets from different accounts that mention the involvement of the Gabriela Women’s Party with the New People’s Army.

Here's our problem formulation.

Research Question

What event has triggered the spread of mis/disinformation about the involvement of Gabriela Women's Party with the New People's Army?

Hypothesis

Mis/disinformation about the association of Gabriela Women's Party with the New People's Army can be correlated with the election of BBM-Sara in the executive branch.

Null Hypothesis

Mis/disinformation about the association of Gabriela Women's Party with the New People's Army cannot be correlated with the election of BBM-Sara in the executive branch.

Action Plan

Analyze the frequency and posting time of mis/disinformation tweets about the Gabriela Women's Party.
